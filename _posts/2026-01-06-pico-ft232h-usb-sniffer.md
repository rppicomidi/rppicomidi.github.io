# [pico-ft232h-usb-sniffer](https://github.com/rppicomidi/pico-ft232h-usb-sniffer)
Use a Raspberry Pi Pico as a USB full speed bus analyzer and use a FT232H board to stream the packet data to Wireshark via high speed USB.
