# [pico-usb-midi-interface](https://github.com/rppicomidi/pico-usb-midi-interface)
8-IN 8-OUT USB device to serial port MIDI interface/patch bay for the Raspberry Pi Pico 2 (RP2350). 6-IN 6-OUT for Pico (RP2040).
